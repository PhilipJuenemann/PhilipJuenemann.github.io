This site hosts Philip's completed computer science projects from CS 180: Intro to Computer Vision and Computational Photography at UC Berkeley, taught by Angjoo Kanazawa and Alexei (Alyosha) Efros.


CS 180 is an advanced undergraduate course focused on computing with visual data, covering image processing, computer vision, and computational photography. The course emphasizes hands-on learning through programming projects where students acquire their own images and develop image analysis and synthesis tools from scratch.
